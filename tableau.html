Seattle Airbnb - Tableau Project

A sneak peek into the Airbnb activity in Seattle, WA, USA. With this dataset, I joined the three tables (listing, calendar, and reviews) available to me as one.

I created my dashboard by designing the following sheets:
  • I used the zipcodes to create an informed decision of best place to buy a home with good return on investment for Airbnb. I used a Bar Chart which included zipcode and AVG(price) for this illustration titled Price by Zipcode.
  • Using generated longitude and latitude, I was able to create a geographical representation using map of neighbourhood by zipcode, with their respective AVG(Price).
  • To find out the best time to put Airbnb on the market for people to use, which was known by finding out when people are spending the most of their money on Airbnb, I used the Date column on calendar with Price to find out the find out the best time to put the Airbnb on the market and titled it Revenue for Year.
  • Also, I created a chart distributing the bedrooms per AVG(price) in order the find out bedrooms that are bringing in the most money.
