Bike Buyers - Excel Project

I was able to perform the following tasks on the dataset:

  • Data Cleaning: removed duplicates, replace letters with words, changed income column to currency, use the IF statement to create age brackets.
  • Looked into the average income of both male and female and the respective sum of genders who purchased a bike or not. I used a Bar Chart for this illustration.
  • By using the commute distance and purchased bike columns, I was able to use a Line Chart to provide customer commute distance and respective total of those who purchased a bike or not.
  • Using the Line with Markers Chart, I was able to categorise by age bracket, age range of customers who has purchased bikes or not.
  • Build Dashboard: I was able to bring together the 3 visualizations in a dashboard called (Bike Sales Dashboard), inserted slicer connected all 3 pivot tables created to filter across the dashboard.
